Nigerian Fried Rice

Description

In Nigeria, fried rice is a well known delicacy usually enjoyed during festive periods. It is the best thing that happened to birthday parties, weddings, street parties, picnics, special events and occasions, but not limited to these occasions.

Fried rice can be cooked in different ways. It is easy to prepare and usually paired with Nigerian salad or coleslaw. It makes you will feel like you are in paradise island.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Green beans
  • Scotch bonnet pepper/Atarado
  • Vegetable oil
  • Shrimps
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Season and boil the chicken, set broth aside.
  2. Chop bell peppers, onions, atarado, onions, green beans.
  3. Thoroughly wash the rice
  4. Add vegetable oil into a pan and fry shrimps and the boiled chicken to preference and set aside.
  5. In a pot, add previous vegetable oil and allow to heat up.
  6. Add washed rice and fry for 10 minutes.
  7. Add chicken stock and water if needed and allow to boil till rice is fully cooked.
  8. In a pan, fry choppped onions and vegetables and set aside.
  9. Add fried shrimps and vegetables to cooked rice and mix.
  10. Allow to cook for an extra 10 minutes.
  11. Add fried chicken, and serve.
